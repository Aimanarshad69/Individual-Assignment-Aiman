ABOUT UiTM

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) is a leading public university in Malaysia, established in 1956. It is renowned for its focus on providing accessible higher education to Bumiputera students, particularly in the fields of science, technology, humanities, and entrepreneurship. UiTM aims to foster a skilled workforce by offering innovative academic programs, research opportunities, and a vibrant campus life. With a mission to produce agile and competent professionals, UiTM also emphasizes the importance of social responsibility and community engagement. Over the years, it has gained recognition globally and remains committed to shaping the future of Malaysia's higher education landscape.
